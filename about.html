About Me

Growing up in Queens, New York, I've always loved art and loved to express myself in abstract and imaginative ways. During my youth, technology was beginning to assimilate into normal people's lives. With that I strived to find a medium that would suit me well. I fell in love with art and technology and being able to learn how to use both in beautiful ways has become everything I've ever wanted.
